    Privacy Policy for Knowledge Vault LXP

    Last Updated: May 24, 2025

    At Knowledge Vault, accessible from www.kvskills.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

    1.1 Information You Provide: Account details, profile information, payment details, and communications.

    1.2 Information Collected Automatically: Usage data, technical data (IP, device, browser), cookies, and tracking data.

    1.3 Information from Educational Institutions: Your institution may provide your details (name, email, student ID, enrollment status).

    2. How We Use Your Information

    1. To provide and maintain the Service.

    2. For personalization of your learning experience.

    3. For operational analysis and quality enhancement.

    4. To communicate important updates and marketing (with your consent).

    5. For security, fraud prevention, and compliance with law.

    3. How We Share Your Information

    1. With educational institutions (progress and performance data).

    2. With service providers (hosting, analytics, payments).

    3. For legal reasons (if required by law).

    4. During business transfers (mergers/acquisitions).

    5. With your consent.

    6. As aggregated or anonymized data for analytics.

    4. Data Security

    We implement safeguards to protect your information, but no method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

    5. Data Retention

    Your data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this policy or as required by law.

    6. Your Privacy Rights

    You may have rights depending on your jurisdiction:

    1. Access your data.

    2. Rectify incorrect data.

    3. Request deletion (Right to be Forgotten).

    4. Restrict or object to processing.

    5. Data portability.

    6. Withdraw consent at any time.

    Our Service may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

    8. Children's Privacy

    We comply with COPPA and relevant laws. Institutions must ensure proper parental consent for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without consent.

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.
